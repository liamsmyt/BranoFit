About Me

Hi! My name is Jack Brannigan and I am a Fitness professional ! I am a certified Personal trainer and Group instructor based in County Kildare.

I have plenty of experience working in the fitness industry and have a huge passion for exercise as you can see by the many qualifications I hold !

I specialise in helping people lose weight, build muscle, get fitter and become the best version of themselves. Putting them in the best shape of their lives!

I believe in a holistic approach to fitness that encompasses physical, mental and emotional wellbeing.

I am a REPs Certified Personal Trainer which means I am officially registered and qualified as a Professional Personal Trainer in Ireland.
